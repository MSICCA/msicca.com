WHO WE ARE

MSICCA | MRVL Engineering & Construction Solutions

We are a multidisciplinary consultancy specialized in engineering, architecture and interior design based in Maracaibo, Venezuela. Since our inception, we have been committed to technical excellence and client satisfaction, delivering integrated solutions that exceed expectations.

Our Mission

To provide integrated engineering, procurement and construction solutions that transform our clients' visions into tangible realities, ensuring quality, efficiency and compliance with international standards in every project.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading EPC solutions consultancy in the Zulia region, expanding our national and international presence while maintaining our commitment to technological innovation and sustainability.

EPC delivery in LATAM

Integrated engineering, procurement, and construction for partners needing reliable execution in Venezuela and the region.

Compliance & safety

NEC/NFPA/OSHA-aligned practices, documented QA/QC, and safety controls suitable for international stakeholders.

Digital coordination

BIM workflows, transparent reporting, and schedule control to keep multi-party projects aligned and auditable.

OUR HISTORY

Growth Trajectory

Since our inception, we have constantly evolved to offer world-class services

  • MSICCA Foundation

    Started operations as an engineering consultancy in Maracaibo, initially specializing in electrical projects and architectural design for the commercial sector.

    2013

  • Service Expansion

    Incorporation of procurement and supply management services, enabling us to offer more complete and integrated solutions to our clients.

    2015

  • Industrial Sector Diversification

    Entry into the industrial market with plant modernization and critical systems projects, expanding our technical capabilities.

    2018

  • International Standards Certification

    Adoption of NEC/NFPA/OSHA standards and DUNS/UEI registration, positioning us to serve international stakeholders and prime contractors.

    2021

  • Digital Transformation

    Implementation of BIM technologies and advanced project management platforms, enhancing our efficiency and collaborative capabilities.

    2024
