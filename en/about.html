Who We Are

MSICCA | Engineering & Construction Solutions

We are a multidisciplinary engineering consultancy based in Maracaibo, Venezuela, specializing in critical infrastructure stabilization and EPC support for international stakeholders operating in complex environments.

Our Mission

To provide rapid-response engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) support that bridges US Federal Standards with local execution capabilities, ensuring operational continuity for international agencies and prime contractors in the Zulia region.

Our Vision

To be the trusted local partner for international organizations requiring compliant, efficient, and transparent engineering services in Venezuela, expanding our capabilities while maintaining our commitment to US standards and ethical business practices.
