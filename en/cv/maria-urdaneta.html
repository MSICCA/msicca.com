María Urdaneta

Field Supervisor | Civil Works Coordinator · MSICCA

Mission Profile

Maria Urdaneta links technical planning and field execution in the Zulia region. Trained as an architect, her operational value is in managing logistics, personnel, and maintenance schedules for critical infrastructure. She provides boots-on-the-ground supervision, ensuring construction and maintenance align with technical specifications and safety protocols, even in resource-constrained environments.

Core Competencies (Operational)

  • Site supervision and QA/QC: oversight of civil works to ensure adherence to drawings and quality standards; primary filter for material inspection and workmanship validation.
  • Infrastructure maintenance logistics: planning and executing large-scale maintenance schedules for transportation infrastructure (roads and bridges), including vegetation control and structural preservation.
  • Technical documentation (as-builts): rapid CAD updates on-site to reflect field changes, maintaining accurate records for federal reporting.
  • Labor management: coordination of local crews and subcontractors, enforcing productivity targets and safety compliance.

Past Performance (Execution & Logistics)

  • Critical transportation infrastructure maintenance: head of maintenance operations for a major regional bridge and highway system; managed weekly deployment schedules across high-traffic corridors to ensure continuity of vital routes.
  • Civil works supervision (private sector): field supervision for multiple construction projects, enforcing calendars and material specifications while managing subcontractor interfaces to deliver on time.
  • Project coordination and vetting: technical coordination for an architecture and engineering firm, overseeing production of technical dossiers and junior staff output to streamline workflow accuracy.

Codes & Classifications

  • Role NAICS: 236220 (Commercial and Institutional Building Construction), 541310 (Architectural Services - Field Support).
  • Capabilities: site logistics, QA/QC inspection, CAD/BIM support, crew supervision.

Education

  • B.Arch. Architecture – Universidad del Zulia.
  • Languages: Spanish (native), English (intermediate - technical).
