Carlos Marval

Lead Technical Drafter | Visualization Specialist · MSICCA

Mission Profile

Carlos Marval leads the Technical Documentation Unit at MSICCA. He transforms raw field data into precise, actionable intelligence. With a background in visual communication and CAD methodologies, he produces high-fidelity as-built drawings, stakeholder renderings, and technical reports that meet the rigorous documentation standards of US Prime Contractors and international agencies.

Core Competencies (Operational Support)

  • Technical visualization and modeling: expert in AutoCAD, SketchUp, and V-Ray to generate 2D/3D representations of complex logistical challenges and infrastructure retrofits.
  • As-built documentation: rapid digitization of site conditions, turning field sketches into professional engineering plans for renovation planning.
  • Infrastructure rehabilitation proposals: conceptualization and visualization of retrofit strategies for dilapidated industrial and urban facilities (brownfields).
  • Visual reporting: high-impact technical presentations and visual aids for project status reports, ensuring clear communication across language barriers.

Past Performance (Technical & Infrastructure)

  • Water management systems design: design and visualization of urban rainwater harvesting infrastructure for low-income settlements, demonstrating capability in sustainable infrastructure for humanitarian contexts.
  • Industrial site repurposing: conceptual master planning for remediation and conversion of decommissioned oil tank farms, showcasing large-scale facility assessment and adaptive reuse planning.
  • Modular housing and shelter systems: visualization and design development for container-based housing units and portable architecture, applicable to rapid-deployment shelters.
  • Urban gateway infrastructure: design of large-scale access control structures and urban landmarks, enabling secure and functional access points for controlled zones.

Codes & Classifications

  • Role NAICS: 541340 (Drafting Services), 541430 (Graphic Design Services - Technical).
  • Capabilities: CAD drafting, 3D modeling, technical illustration, site mapping visualization.

Education

  • B.Arch. Architecture – Universidad del Zulia.
