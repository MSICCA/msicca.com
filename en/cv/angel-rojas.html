Ángel Rojas

Director of Maintenance & Grid Operations | M.S. Maintenance Management · MSICCA

Mission Profile

Angel Rojas leads MSICCA’s efforts in Critical Infrastructure Continuity. With a Master’s degree in Maintenance Management and over 15 years of experience, he specializes in stabilizing power grids and hardening high-voltage systems. He manages large-scale field operations in high-stress environments, ensuring mission-critical infrastructure meets performance and safety requirements of international agencies and global prime contractors.

Core Competencies (Strategic Support)

  • Grid stabilization & HV/MV engineering: oversight of substation maintenance and power distribution circuits (up to 115 kV/230 kV).
  • Asset lifecycle management (SAP PM): data-driven maintenance strategies to mitigate risks and optimize procurement for critical parts.
  • Testing & commissioning (NACE/IEEE standards): diagnostic testing of transformers (oil/dry), circuit breakers, and grounding systems using advanced instrumentation.
  • Procurement & compliance: experience in public/private bidding, ensuring technical and financial compliance within complex regulatory frameworks.

Past Performance (Technical & Field Leadership)

  • Power distribution infrastructure reconstruction: lead engineer for construction and maintenance of 24 kV distribution circuits and power substations; restored and improved grid reliability for critical service areas.
  • Field leadership in hostile/austere environments: managed multidisciplinary teams (60+ personnel) for emergency power restoration and preventative maintenance; maintained KPIs under severe grid instability.
  • Industrial hardening & backup power deployment: EPC of power generation plants and advanced grounding systems for sensitive equipment; guaranteed 24/7 mission continuity for industrial and high-security facilities.
  • Technical compliance & contract management: evaluated and drafted technical specifications for electrical contracts; streamlined vendor selection and ensured QA/QC throughout project lifecycle.

Technical Classifications

  • Role NAICS: 221122 (Electric Power Distribution), 811310 (Commercial and Industrial Machinery Maintenance).
  • Technical proficiency: SAP PM, ETAP (Power Systems Analysis), specialized field testing (CPC-100, CT Analyzer).

Education

  • M.S. Maintenance Management – Universidad del Zulia.
  • B.S. Electrical Engineering – Universidad del Zulia.
  • Vetting: documented history in utility-scale management and compliance; no political exposure.
